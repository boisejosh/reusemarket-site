Spring is here, and it's the perfect time to sort through those art supplies, fabric scraps, and craft materials that have been piling up over the winter.

## How to Donate

Drop off your clean, reusable materials any **Tuesday in May**, from **10:00 a.m. to 12:30 p.m.**

We're located upstairs in North End Organic Nursery:
**3777 W Chinden Blvd., Garden City, ID 83714**

## What We're Looking For

- Fabric scraps, yarn, ribbon, and sewing notions
- Paper, cardstock, foam board, and specialty papers
- Art supplies — brushes, paints, markers (working condition)
- Craft materials — beads, buttons, wire, mixed media
- Design samples — tile, wallpaper, fabric swatches

Please make sure everything is **clean, dry, and in usable condition**. We can accept up to two boxes per visit. For larger donations, [contact us](/contact) to arrange a pickup.

## Not Sure If We'll Take It?

Check our [Donation Guidelines](/donate/guidelines) page, or email us at [info@reusemarket.org](mailto:info@reusemarket.org) and we'll let you know.

Thank you for helping keep these materials out of the landfill and in the hands of creators!
