We're excited to launch our newly rebuilt website! The ReUse Market has been serving the Boise community since 2006, and we wanted a site that better reflects what we're all about: creativity, community, and keeping good materials out of the landfill.

## What's New

- **Easier navigation** — find hours, donation guidelines, and volunteer info in just a click or two
- **News & Events section** — you're reading our very first post!
- **Online volunteer sign-up** — interested in helping out? Fill out the form on our Volunteer page
- **Donation guidelines** — clear info on what we accept (and what we can't)

## Still the Same Mission

Our location, hours, and mission haven't changed. We're still upstairs in North End Organic Nursery at 3777 W Chinden Blvd., Garden City — open Thursday through Saturday, noon to 5 p.m.

As always, please check our [Hours & Location](/hours) page before visiting, since our hours depend on volunteer availability.
